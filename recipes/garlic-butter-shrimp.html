Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Chopped parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  2. Add shrimp to the skillet, season with salt, pepper, and paprika, and cook until the shrimp are pink, about 3 minutes per side.
  3. Stir in lemon juice and cook for another 1 minute.
  4. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
