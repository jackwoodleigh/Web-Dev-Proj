Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, etc.)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  3. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly.
  4. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and grilled chicken slices.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing and toss to coat.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese if desired and serve immediately.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, marinate the chicken in olive oil, lemon, and herbs for 30 minutes before grilling.
  • Add avocado slices for a creamy texture.
