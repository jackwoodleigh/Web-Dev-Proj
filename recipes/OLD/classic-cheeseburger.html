Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)
  • Butter (for toasting buns)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties, making a small indentation in the center of each patty.
  3. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or longer if desired.
  5. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and let it melt.
  6. Meanwhile, butter the buns and toast them lightly on the grill or in a pan.
  7. Assemble the burger by placing the cooked patty on the bottom bun, and adding onion, tomato, pickles, and your preferred condiments.
  8. Top with the other bun and serve. Enjoy!
