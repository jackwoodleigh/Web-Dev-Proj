Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add diced chicken and cook until browned.
  2. Add onion, peas, and carrots, and cook until softened.
  3. Push the chicken and vegetables to one side of the pan, then add the eggs to the other side and scramble.
  4. Add the rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil, and stir to combine.
  5. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, then serve hot.

Tips

  • For an extra kick, add a dash of hot sauce or chili flakes to the fried rice.
  • Feel free to add any vegetables you have on hand, like bell peppers, corn, or broccoli.
  • Use day-old rice for better texture in fried rice.
