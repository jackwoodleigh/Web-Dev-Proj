Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk, then dredge in the flour mixture.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Fry chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 12-15 minutes.
  5. Let the chicken rest on a paper towel-lined plate before serving.

Tips

  • For extra crispy chicken, double-dip the chicken by dipping it in buttermilk and flour twice.
  • If you don't have buttermilk, you can make a substitute by adding 1 tbsp of lemon juice to 1 cup of milk and letting it sit for 5 minutes.
  • For even juicier chicken, marinate the pieces in buttermilk for several hours or overnight before frying.
