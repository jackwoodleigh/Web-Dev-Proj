Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced bell peppers
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, shredded cheese, cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, saut√© bell peppers, onion, and garlic over medium heat until softened.
  2. Add beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with toppings.

Tips

  • Add a splash of lime juice for a citrusy kick.
  • Top with sliced avocado for a creamy texture.
  • Use roasted vegetables for extra depth of flavor.
