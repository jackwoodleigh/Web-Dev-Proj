Spinach and Feta Quiche

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, sauté spinach until wilted. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  4. Spread the sautéed spinach and crumbled feta into the pie crust.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach and feta.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, until the quiche is set and lightly golden.
  7. Allow to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Tips

  • Use fresh spinach for the best flavor, or frozen spinach (thawed and drained) as an alternative.
  • If you don’t have heavy cream, you can substitute with half-and-half or milk for a lighter version.
