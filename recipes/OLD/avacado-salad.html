Avocado Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice, then toss gently to combine.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve immediately.

Tips

  • Add a handful of corn or black beans for extra flavor and protein.
  • If making ahead, store the salad in an airtight container with some lime juice to prevent browning.
  • For extra crunch, add some tortilla chips or toasted nuts on top before serving.
