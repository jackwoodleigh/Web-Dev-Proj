Avocado Toast with Egg

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat or sourdough)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil (for toasting the bread)
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes, lemon juice, or herbs

Instructions

  1. Toast the slices of bread in a pan with olive oil until crispy and golden brown.
  2. Meanwhile, mash the ripe avocado in a bowl, seasoning it with salt, pepper, and optional lemon juice or red pepper flakes.
  3. Fry or poach the eggs to your preference.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado generously on the toasted bread.
  5. Top with the cooked eggs and garnish with herbs or red pepper flakes.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips

  • For extra creaminess, add a dollop of Greek yogurt or cottage cheese to the mashed avocado.
  • To make the toast even healthier, opt for whole grain or gluten-free bread.
  • Experiment with different toppings, like sliced tomatoes, radishes, or a sprinkle of chia seeds for extra nutrients.
  • For an extra protein boost, consider adding a few slices of smoked salmon on top of the eggs.
