Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder (optional for fluffier pancakes)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • Butter or oil for greasing the pan
  • Optional toppings: maple syrup, fresh berries, or nuts

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
  2. Whisk the eggs into the mashed bananas. Add vanilla extract, baking powder, and cinnamon if desired.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat, greasing it with butter or oil.
  4. Pour the banana batter onto the skillet in small circles, cooking for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve the pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or nuts.

Tips

  • For thicker pancakes, use less mashed banana or add a tablespoon of flour or oat flour.
  • If you prefer fluffier pancakes, add the baking powder and a splash of milk to the batter.
  • Use a non-stick skillet to avoid sticking, especially since these pancakes are more delicate than regular ones.
  • Top with peanut butter or almond butter for extra protein and flavor.
  • Serve with a side of scrambled eggs or bacon for a complete breakfast.
