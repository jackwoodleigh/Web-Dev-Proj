Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 12 taco shells (hard or soft)
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Optional toppings: sliced jalapenos, cilantro, avocado, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water, and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  3. While the beef is cooking, heat taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Once the beef mixture is ready, fill each taco shell with the seasoned beef.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, cheese, and other toppings of your choice.
  6. Serve immediately with a side of salsa and sour cream.

Tips

  • If you prefer leaner tacos, substitute ground turkey or chicken for the beef.
  • For a crunchier shell, bake the taco shells in the oven for a few minutes before filling them.
  • Add a squeeze of lime over the tacos for an extra burst of flavor.
  • For a vegetarian option, swap the meat for seasoned black beans or lentils.
  • Use your favorite hot sauce for extra spice!
