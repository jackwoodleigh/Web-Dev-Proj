Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 oz dark chocolate
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and grease 4 ramekins.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together in a microwave-safe bowl, then mix in the flour, salt, and powdered sugar.
  3. Whisk in eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes until edges are firm, but the center is soft.
  5. Let cool for a minute, then carefully flip out onto plates. Serve immediately with ice cream or whipped cream.
