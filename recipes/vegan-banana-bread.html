Vegan Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the mashed bananas, almond milk, maple syrup, and melted coconut oil.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir to combine.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, add some chopped nuts or chocolate chips to the batter.
  • If you don't have almond milk, any plant-based milk will work.
  • Let the banana bread cool completely before slicing to prevent it from being too crumbly.
