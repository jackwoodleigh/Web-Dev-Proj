Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken cubes with yogurt, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, chili powder, coriander, and a pinch of salt. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Cook for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Reduce the heat, add the cream, and stir to combine. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Serve with basmati rice or naan. Enjoy!

Tips

  • For a spicier dish, add extra chili powder or a chopped green chili.
  • Try adding fresh cilantro on top for a fresh burst of flavor.
