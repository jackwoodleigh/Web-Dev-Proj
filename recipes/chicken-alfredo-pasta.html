Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add garlic to the skillet and saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Let the mixture simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked pasta, and garnish with chopped parsley.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • Use half-and-half instead of heavy cream for a lighter sauce.
  • Substitute chicken with shrimp or mushrooms for a different twist.
