Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the broccoli, bell peppers, carrot, and zucchini to the pan. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, and stir to coat the vegetables evenly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, sprinkle with sesame seeds for extra flavor and crunch.
  6. Serve immediately with rice or noodles. Enjoy!

Tips

  • Feel free to add other vegetables like mushrooms, snap peas, or baby corn for variety.
  • If you prefer a spicier stir fry, you can add a dash of chili flakes or sriracha sauce.
