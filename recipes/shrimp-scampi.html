Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz linguine pasta
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Chopped parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the linguine pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Add the white wine and lemon juice, then simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Toss in the cooked pasta and mix everything together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Tips

  • Use fresh shrimp for the best flavor. If using frozen, make sure to thaw them completely before cooking.
  • For a richer sauce, add more butter or a splash of heavy cream.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes while cooking the garlic.
  • Serve with a side of garlic bread or a light salad to complete the meal.
